# Privacy Policy

**Effective:** October 26, 2023

## Information Collection

The site collects three types of data:

- **Submitted information**: Names, emails, and form details provided by visitors
- **Usage data**: Pages visited, links clicked, and browsing behavior
- **Tracking technologies**: Cookies and similar tools to enhance the user experience

## Data Usage

Collected information is used to:

- Deliver and enhance services
- Answer customer inquiries
- Customize website experiences
- Send newsletters (with permission)

## Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except as necessary for providing our services or with your consent.

## Protection Measures

The site employs reasonable security measures to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure of personal data.

## User Rights

Visitors can access, correct, or delete their personal information by contacting the site owner.

## Policy Updates

The privacy policy may be updated periodically. Users should review it regularly for changes.

## Contact

Questions or concerns should be directed to the contact email on this site.
