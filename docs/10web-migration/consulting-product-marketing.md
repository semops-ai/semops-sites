# Product & Marketing

- **Market & Strategy Alignment:** Market research & analysis and defining precise product requirements that directly support your business objectives, ensuring every initiative contributes to your strategic vision. I call what I do "Technical Marketing". I introduce mechanisms that use a hypothesis mindset to use data and make judgement calls with more intention and goal orientation.

- **Cross-Functional Collaboration:** Navigating complex organizational structures and fostering teamwork and cooperation across diverse departments. Through concrete metrics and tools (including AI), promoting efficient execution and alignment towards common goals.

- **Performance Measurement:** Establishing clear Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) and/or evaluating existing metrics. Designing and developing processes and data systems (see "Data & Analytics" below) to track progress, measure success, and facilitate data-informed adjustments.

- **Data-Driven Strategic Insights:** Employing rigorous data analysis techniques to inform critical strategic decisions, optimize marketing spend, and drive improvements in your products and customer experiences.
